Platform features

Built for Real Communication

Every feature in Zynochat exists for a reason — to make your conversations faster, safer, and more meaningful. Here is everything the platform has to offer.

Core messaging

Real-Time Messaging at Any Scale

Zynochat's messaging engine delivers messages in under 2ms average latency — built on a distributed WebSocket infrastructure designed to handle millions of concurrent connections without degradation.

  • ✦ Instant delivery with read receipts and typing indicators
  • ✦ Message threading for organized conversations
  • ✦ Message editing, deletion, and reaction emojis
  • ✦ Offline message queue — never miss a message

End-to-End Encryption

Every private conversation is encrypted from your device to the recipient's — not even Zynochat can read your messages. Uses industry-standard X25519 key exchange and XSalsa20-Poly1305 encryption.

Communities & Channels

Create communities with multiple topic channels, voice rooms, and announcement boards. Set granular permissions for each role — from guest to administrator — with fine-grained control.

Mobile Native App

The Zynochat app is available for Android and iOS with full feature parity. Optimized for battery life and data efficiency — designed for users who are always on the move.

Rich Media Sharing

Share images, videos, audio clips, documents, and files up to 2GB per transfer. Auto-generated previews, in-chat media playback, and organized media galleries per conversation.

Powerful Search

Full-text search across all your messages, media, and communities. Filter by date, sender, or channel. Find any conversation in under a second — no matter how far back it goes.

Clean, Focused UI

Zynochat's interface is designed to keep you in the conversation — not fighting the app. No cluttered menus, no distracting ads. Just clean, beautiful design that adapts to any screen.

Smart Notifications

Granular notification controls per community and channel. Set quiet hours, keyword alerts, and priority contacts. Never miss what matters; always mute what doesn't.

Moderation Tools

Comprehensive moderation suite for community owners: user bans, message filtering, spam detection, audit logs, and automated rules. Keep your space safe and welcoming.

Cross-Platform Sync

One account, all your devices. Messages, media, and read states sync in real time across your phone, tablet, and browser. Pick up exactly where you left off, every time.

Download Zynochat Today

Available for Android, iOS, and the web. Free to get started — no credit card needed.

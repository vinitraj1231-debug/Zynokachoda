How to Create a Group in Zynochat — Step-by-Step Guide (2026)

Setting up a group in Zynochat takes under a minute. Here is the complete guide from creation to community management.

  • Open zynochat.in and log in to your account
  • Tap the Groups icon in the bottom navigation bar
  • Tap "+ New Group" — the creation screen opens
  • Enter a Group Name, Description, and upload a Group Photo
  • Search and add members by username or email
  • Choose privacy — Public (open join) or Private (invite-only)
  • Tap "Create Group" — your group is live instantly!

Admin Tools

  • Pin messages — keep key info at the top for all members
  • Assign moderators — delegate management to trusted members
  • Slow mode — limit message frequency during busy events
  • Invite links — generate shareable links with optional expiry

How to Secure Your Chats on Zynochat — Privacy Checklist (2026)

Zynochat has strong built-in security — these steps will make your account and conversations even more private.

Security Checklist

  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication — Settings → Security → 2FA. Use Google Authenticator or Authy. Save backup codes securely.
  • Use a Strong Unique Password — Minimum 12 characters, mixed case, numbers, symbols. Use a password manager.
  • Review Active Sessions — Settings → Security → Active Sessions. Revoke any unfamiliar devices instantly.
  • Configure Privacy Settings — Set "Who can message me" to Contacts Only. Restrict profile photo and Last Seen visibility.
  • Enable Disappearing Messages — Auto-delete sensitive conversations after 1 hour, 1 day, or 7 days.
  • Enable Screen Lock — Settings → Security → Screen Lock. Require biometric or PIN to open the app.

How to Backup Chats on Zynochat — Complete Guide (2026)

How to Recover Messages on Zynochat — Recovery Guide (2026)

Lost chat history? Here is exactly what you can do for every common scenario.

Switched Devices

Simply log in at zynochat.in — messages sync automatically from the cloud within seconds.

Accidentally Deleted a Message

"Delete for Everyone" is permanent by design — this is a privacy feature. "Delete for Me" only removes the message from your view; it still exists on the other person's device. Always use "Delete for Me" unless you specifically need both sides cleared.

Account Compromised

  • Use Forgot Password at zynochat.in immediately
  • Once inside, cloud sync should restore your message history
  • Go to Active Sessions and revoke all unrecognised devices
  • Enable 2FA right away to prevent recurrence

How to Fix Zynochat Login Issues — Troubleshooting Guide (2026)

Every common Zynochat login problem has a straightforward fix. Here is the complete troubleshooting guide.

Wrong Password

  • Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
  • Enter your registered email (check spam if the reset email doesn't appear)
  • Click the reset link and create a new strong password

Email Not Recognised

Try all email addresses you may have used. Check for typos — "gmail.com" vs "gamil.com" is common. Contact Zynochat support if you still cannot identify the account.

2FA Code Not Working

Ensure your phone's clock is synced — 2FA codes are time-based and expire after 30 seconds. Use a saved backup code if available. Contact support with identity documents if access is lost entirely.

Page Not Loading

  • Clear browser cache and cookies, then retry
  • Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
  • Switch between Wi-Fi and mobile data
  • Wait a few minutes — temporary service interruptions are rare but possible
